Group reflection

What went well

Communication is really important in group work. In this group assignment we were very prompt in communicating with each other. We will have our weekly video meeting on MS Teams on time to present our respective points of view. After the meetings, when we come across parts that need to be communicated, we also communicate in discord and exchange ideas. All these made it easier and more efficient for us to complete this group assignment.

What could be improved

For this assignment we needed to make a prototype of the application and we chose to use Figma to make the prototype. However, none of the members of our group were very familiar with Figma and had to go through tutorials to learn it, but we spent so much time on the text content earlier that we ended up not spending much time on Figma and had to compress our time to complete our prototype. This is very bad and we should have studied the tutorials in advance rather than waiting until we were about to start working on it, resulting in a very tight schedule at the end. In future assignments, similar problems must not occur again.

At least one thing that was surprising

Surprisingly, we were able to complete the prototype in a satisfactory manner without having much time left. At the beginning, everyone was not very familiar with using Figma and was not very happy with the first page design. We needed more specific and logical details about the functionality of the page and more plugins and patterns to make the page more colourful. We decided to finish the group meeting by learning how to use Figma ourselves on the internet and borrowing some templates that would make our task easier. In the end, after more discussions and based on what we had learnt and understood, we were able to improve the prototype page more and more.

At least one thing that you have learned about groups

What we have learned about group work is that it is very important to communicate ideas and suggestions to each other in a timely manner. Because one person's knowledge is limited, but the combined knowledge of the whole group has unlimited potential. This is what group work is all about. Through communication, we filled in and drew on each other's expertise to complete a detailed description of such a huge project.
