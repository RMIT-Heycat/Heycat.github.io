Skills & Job

An application development team should have product manager, UI designer, front- end engineer, back-end engineer and test engineer. Since I was funded by venture capitalists to develop this project, I will assume the position of product manager and hire the remaining four positions mentioned above.

UI Designer

  • Have over 3 year experience in UX and UI design.
  • Education in a related course, at least undergraduate
  • A deep understanding of user-centered design
  • Conduct user research, interviews and surveys, and use the information to create wireframes and prototype.
  • User experience and interaction design for responsive websites and mobile apps (iOS & Android)
  • Data analysis with business intelligence and analytics tools such as Google Analytics or similar
  • Proficient with Sketch, Figma or Adobe XD and other rapid prototyping tools.
  • Exceptional creative skills
  • Have outstanding verbal and written communication.
  • A forward thinking, innovative attitude, coming up with solutions for improve and make things better

Front-end Engineer

  • A Programming / Software Development related University degree.
  • 3+ years of experience in a software development role.
  • Proficient understanding of HTML5, CSS3.
  • Proficient understanding of git.
  • A solid understanding of front end concepts & technologies, such as vanilla Javascript, SaSS.
  • Proficient understanding of client-side scripting and JavaScript frameworks, including jQuery.
  • Good understanding of app frameworks like Angular and ReactJS.
  • Knowledge of open source libraries, tools and frameworks.
  • Basic knowledge of image authoring tools and familiarity with tools such as Photoshop.
  • Sound understanding of information architecture: wireframes, mud maps, node diagrams.
  • Architecting high performance and scalable applications.
  • Extremely high-attention to details.
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Ability to work independently.
  • Ability to work collaboratively as part of a small team in a fast-paced environment.
